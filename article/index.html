ETC texture format

As of 2016, comes in two flavors : ETC1, the original, RGB only (2005) ETC2, more quality + sRGB + RGBA mandatory in OpenGL ES 3.0 and OpenGL 4.3 More info on wikipedia. Platform support ETC1 Android: 2.2+ ETC2 Android: 4.3+, Nexus 4+, Galaxy S5 iOS: iOS 7+, iPhone 5s+ Tools (ETC1) Globally found nothing good, to build KTX files, from bad to worse : PVRTexTool No TIF support as input Awfully slow with default settings, use -q pvrtcfast Mali GPU Texture compression tool http://malideveloper.arm.com/resources/tools/mali-gpu-texture-compression-tool ?
